NEW YORK CITY

HELPING TO BAN SINGLE-USE PLASTIC STRAWS

Helping to ban single-use plastic straws In May 2018, we partnered with our friends over at the New York Aquarium to help spread the word and pass a ban on single-use plastic straws in NYC. The campaign acquired enough buzz to gain attention from local legislators and congress members to continue pushing for the bill to pass. The awareness created, and discussions elicited, far exceeded the initial dollar spend for the campaign and empowered New Yorkers to take action on a cause worth believing in.

USA

SPREADING “GOOD NEWS”

Today media is used to perpetuate fear. From worldwide suffering, to hurricanes, politics, and mass murders, we are constantly bombarded with fear and negativity. In 2017, we partnered with our friends at BlindKnight Studios and helped them create a name/brand positioning for their new media outlet we called “Freequency”. The sole purpose of Freequency was to highlight the positive changes that are taking place in and around our communities in the USA, via an intimate-styled interview hosted by Jannle Gueits.
