OUR PEOPLE

THOSE WHO FOLLOW THE CROWD GET LOST IN IT

Our team is always looking to grow, get out of their comfort zone, and take charge.
It's this type of attitude that helps accelerate our client's business. Meet our growing team.

ERIC ROJAS

CCO/FOUNDER

Eric Rojas is dedicated to building relationships with brands and their consumers that goes beyond the purchase of a product.

Eric has won numerous creative awards and has been featured in Creativity, AdWeek, Billboard —and Forbes interviewed him for “The Power of Purpose” article series that highlights how purpose drives business and social impact.

As a former Creative Director for Havas Worldwide, Ammirati and Deutsch his clients have included; Ikea, Tylenol, Coca-Cola, Starwood Hotels, Reckitt Benckiser, Liberty Mutual, Charles Schwab, New York Life, Prudential, Revlon, Olympus and more. He’s been challenging advertising industry conventions throughout his career and has helped launch some of the biggest brands in their categories, such as vitaminwater, smartwater and Pirate’s Booty.

In 2018, Eric helped create a non-profit organization called “For The Greater Hood” which helps to bring warmth and human connection to the most marginalized group in the NYC community: the homeless.

EMILY MARTIN

ACCOUNT LEAD

Emily hopes to make it big on Shark Tank one day. She's seen every episode, but she isn't a dreamer. Emily is a down-to-earth realist that wants to get things done as efficiently as possible. By building relationships with clients and business partners alike, she strategically oversees the work and sets the stage for creativity to thrive. Creative brainstorms are both exhilarating and taxing for her, because her brain is like a browser with 20 open tabs testing the practicality of it all. We'd call her a shark, but she's too sweet.

ALLA GONOPOLSKY

STRATEGY DIRECTOR

Like anyone with the word "strategy" in their title, Alla can often be found explaining Venn diagrams to sound smart in meetings. For the last 12 years, she has strategized on big global brands and scrappy upstarts across creative shops in New York and San Francisco (i.e. Volvo, MINI, Lysol, Keurig, and Samsung) Otherwise, she's wherever her passport hasn't been stamped yet, playing her bright green ukulele and doing yoga in airports.

RICHARD NOTARIANNI

PLANNING DIRECTOR

A pioneer in art and science of integrated communications strategy and operations, Richard has designed and implemented breakthrough efforts from TD Ameritrade, Schwab, Dos Equis, Jaguar, Liberty Mutual, Nestle, Exxon Mobil, Merck, Lockheed Martin (Space Day), and Hershey’s to name just a few.

JOSHUA BULL

SENIOR ART DIRECTOR + DESIGN LEAD

Joshua believes design is far too important to take lightly. Employing a thoughtful approach, he’s developed a reputation for consistently creating work that cuts through the clutter. It’s also earned him the rarefied dual status as a verb. He’s frequently asked to ‘Josh-ify’ things to make them beautiful.

Joshua also enjoys breaking things. Or at least he did when he was a kid. He spent his teenage years breaking down computers and rebuilding them because curiosity always drove him to ask, 'how does this work?'

And sure, he got into trouble, but he also learned a lot about the power of asking 'why', and how passion and persistence can nearly always yield awesome results.

EMMA WOOD

SENIOR ART DIRECTOR

Emma is an Art Director by trade, and a Real Housewives fan by choice. A lover of bold fonts, muted colors, a well designed menu (that includes burrata and/or mezcal), and shoes. She’s on an endless quest to find her next creative outlet, and will pretty much try anything on her way — as long as there is pizza involved.

FRANKIE QUINONES

DIGITAL LEAD

At Six+One, Frankie leads the charge with everything digital. From strategy development and multi-channel campaign creation, to overall digital marketing and analytics, he specializes in successfully navigating organizations through the digital transformation landscape, as well as increasing ROI within our agency roster. He prefers a challenging game of chess over an easy foe, and in similar fashion, loves projects that require him to grow and dig deep. He believes you can’t excel in leadership without first excelling as a servant. And as far as Frankie is concerned, loving God, loving people, and training up the next generation are what it’s all about.

DIOR RODRIGUEZ

SENIOR EDITOR

Dior is a storyteller. Technically speaking, she is an editor, and a director. But those are just the means by which she tells her stories. The most important tools at her disposal are her eyes, ears, and her heart. It’s how she listens to the world as she travels the continents, talking to strangers, and collecting stories. Dior spends practically all her free time editing. And she just discovered a love of the sauna. If she can figure out a way to edit in the sauna, she'll be the happiest woman in the world.
